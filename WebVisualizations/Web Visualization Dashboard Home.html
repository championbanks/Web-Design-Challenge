Web Visualization Dashboard

This website enables the visualization the weather of 500+ cities across the world of varying distance from the equator. It is utilizing a [simple Python library](https://pypi.python.org/pypi/citipy), the [OpenWeatherMap API](https://openweathermap.org/api), and a little common sense to create a representative model of weather across world cities.

The objective is to build a series of scatter plots to showcase the following relationships: * Temperature (F) vs. Latitude * Humidity (%) vs. Latitude * Cloudiness (%) vs. Latitude * Wind Speed (mph) vs. Latitude
